## Why Must It Be Global?

### Success in One Nation Is Not Enough

**Reality:**
```
Single country transformation successful
    ‚Üì

- Capital flight
- International sanctions
- Technology blockade
- Military threats
- Migration pressure
    ‚Üì

```

**Historical Lessons:**
```

- Attempted to build socialism alone

- Drained by arms race
```

    <li><strong>Global Governance</strong>: Do you accept global parliament and court? Or feel it violates sovereignty?</li>
    <li><strong>Civilizational Identity</strong>: Do you identify as "Earth citizen"? Or feel it's too abstract?</li>
    <li><strong>Time Patience</strong>: Can you accept 100-200 years of global integration?</li>
    <li><strong>Conflict Risk</strong>: Will globalization lead to civilizational conflicts? How to avoid?</li>
  </ol>
</div>
