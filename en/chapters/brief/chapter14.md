### OmniHelp's Emotional Module Functions

#### Function 1: Emotion Recognition and Response

**How It Works:**
```
You: I'm so tired today, don't want to talk.
    â†“

**Key:**

#### Function 2: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

**Common Distortion Types:**

```

    â†“

**2. All-or-Nothing Thinking**
```

    â†“
AI identifies: All-or-nothing thinking
    â†“
AI challenges:

    â†“

```

## Layer 3: Professional Mental Health Services

### Comprehensive Mental Health System

**Principle:**
> Mental health is as important as physical health

---

### Service Levels

#### Level 1: Prevention and Education

**Universal Mental Health Education:**
- Schools: Teach emotion management from elementary school
- Community: Regular mental health lectures
- Media: Mental health knowledge dissemination

**Goals:**
- De-stigmatize (mental health issues aren't shameful)
- Early identification (know when to seek help)
- Self-care (daily mental health maintenance)

---

#### Level 2: Primary Psychological Counseling

**Community Counselors:**
- Every community equipped with counselors
- Free or low cost
- Convenient appointments (online or in-person)

**Services:**
- Listening and empathy
- Cognitive behavioral techniques
- Stress management
- Relationship guidance

**For:**
- Daily stress, anxiety
- Mild depression
- Relationship issues
- Life transitions

---

#### Level 3: Professional Psychotherapy

**Clinical Psychologists, Psychiatrists:**
- For more serious mental health issues
- Long-term therapy
- Multiple modalities (CBT, psychoanalysis, family therapy, etc.)

**Services:**
- Moderate to severe depression
- Anxiety disorders
- Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
- Personality disorders
- Medication (if needed)

**All free (basic guarantee, Chapter 13)**

---

#### Level 4: Crisis Intervention

**24/7 Mental Health Crisis Hotline:**
- Phone, text, online chat
- Trained crisis intervention specialists
- Can immediately connect emergency resources

**Mobile Crisis Teams:**
- Can come to you (if necessary)
- Assess risk
- Provide immediate support
- Connect follow-up services

**For:**
- Suicide risk
- Acute psychotic episodes
- Severe trauma
- Domestic violence victims

---

### Diversity of Treatment Modalities

**One size doesn't fit all:**

**Provide Choices:**
- Talk therapy (psychotherapy)
- Medication (antidepressants, anti-anxiety)
- Mind-body therapies (yoga, meditation, tai chi)
- Art therapy (painting, music, dance)
- Group therapy
- Family therapy

**Personalized Plans:**
- AI + Expert assessment
- What works best for you
- Can combine multiple methods
