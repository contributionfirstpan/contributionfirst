## Fundamental Problems with Current Economic Systems

### Five Defects of Capitalism

#### Defect 1: Exploitation and Inequality

**Core Mechanism:**
```

    â†“
Workers only have labor power
    â†“

    â†“

    â†“

    â†“

```

**Result:**
- Wealth concentration: 1% owns 50% of global wealth
- Wealth gap: Continuously widening
- Worker poverty: Work hard but remain poor
- Capitalist wealth: Unearned income

---

```

    â†“

    â†“
```

## Forms of Production Organization

### Four Main Forms Coexist

#### Form 1: Worker Cooperatives

**Definition:**
> Enterprises collectively owned and democratically managed by workers.

**How It Works:**
```
Ownership: Workers collectively own
    â†“
Management: One person one vote, democratic decisions
    â†“
Profits: Distributed by labor (not by capital)
    â†“
Decisions: Major issues voted by all
```

**Advantages:**
- No exploitation (workers are own bosses)
- Democratic (everyone has voice)
- Stable (won't easily lay off)
- Long-term focused (not just quarterly profits)

**Real Case:**

**Mondragon Corporation (Spain):**
- Founded: 1956
- Scale: 100,000 workers, hundreds of enterprises
- Principles: Democracy, solidarity, equality
- Results:
  - During economic crisis, shared burden (wage cuts not layoffs)
  - Wage gap: Highest not exceeding lowest by 6 times
  - Bankruptcy rate far below traditional enterprises
  - Continuous innovation

**Contribution-First Support:**
- Cooperatives priority access to resources
- Cooperative workers earn additional contribution value
- AI helps cooperative management (democratic decision support)

---

#### Form 2: Public Enterprises

**Applicable Fields:**
- Natural monopolies (water, electricity, networks)
- Infrastructure (railways, ports)
- Basic services (healthcare, education)
- Strategic industries (energy, communications)

**Why Public?**
```
These fields:
    â†“
Not suitable for competition (duplicate construction wasteful)
    â†“
Related to public interest
    â†“
Cannot pursue profit maximization
    â†“
Should be public
```

**How It Works:**
- Ownership: All people
- Management: Professional managers (but democratically supervised)
- Goal: Serve public (not profit)
- Oversight:
  - Citizens' Assembly (Chapter 17)
  - Regular audits
  - Performance evaluation (service quality, efficiency)

**Example:**
```
Electric company:
    â†“
Goals:
- Guarantee everyone's electricity (not profit maximization)
- Clean energy (ecological priority)
- Reasonable prices (cover costs + improvements)
    â†“
Management:
- Professional team operates
- Citizen oversight
- AI optimizes grid
```

---

#### Form 3: Small Private Businesses

**Allowed Scope:**
- Small scale (employees <20)
- Non-strategic industries
- Personal creativity, crafts

**Why Allow?**
- Flexible, innovative
- Entrepreneurial spirit
- Diversification

**Restrictions:**
```
Cannot:
- Expand infinitely (exceeds scale â†’ convert to cooperative)
- Exploit workers (minimum wage guarantee)
- Monopolize market
- Damage environment
```

**Conversion Mechanism:**
```
Business grows to 20 people
    â†“
Choice:
1. Convert to worker cooperative (workers gain ownership)
2. Maintain 20-person scale
    â†“
Cannot: Continue capitalist-style expansion by hiring
```

**Examples:**
- Restaurants, cafes
- Art studios
- Craft workshops
- Consulting services

---

#### Form 4: Open Source/Commons Projects

**Applicable Fields:**
- Software, knowledge, culture
- Collective creation
- No need for exclusivity

**How It Works:**
```
Creators:
    â†“
Open-source release (everyone can use)
    â†“
Others:
- Free use
- Improve
- Reshare
    â†“
Collective wisdom accumulates
```

**Incentives:**
- Not monopoly profits
- But:
  - Contribution value (social recognition)
  - Reputation
  - Others' improvements also benefit self

**Cases:**
- Linux operating system
- Wikipedia
- Open-source AI models
- Scientific papers (open access)

**Contribution-First Enhancement:**
- Open-source contribution = High contribution value
- Users pay small contribution value (support creators)
- But still free to use
